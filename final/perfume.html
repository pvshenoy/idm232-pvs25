Dior Addict

EAU FRAICHE, 2004

★★★★ ★

Dior Addict Eau Fraiche 2004 by Dior is a Amber Floral fragrance for women. Dior Addict Eau Fraiche 2004 was launched in 2004. The nose behind this fragrance is Thierry Wasser. Top notes are Orange Blossom, Vanilla, Mandarin Leaf, Mandarin Orange, Orange and Bergamot; middle notes are Gardenia, Tuberose, Freesia, Jasmine and Bulgarian Rose; base notes are Tonka Bean, Brazilian Rosewood and Sandalwood.
